#  Merchant

## How to Become a Merchant
Being a **Merchant** allows you to have an active role on the platform: you can create your own public orders and accept exchange requests from other users.

### Registration Steps:
1.  **Locate the access:** On the marketplace main page, find and click the **“Become a Merchant”** button.
2.  **Complete the form:** A modal will open where you must deposit a **collateral** in USDC (or the stablecoin configured by the protocol).
3.  **Verify your balance:** Check your available balance in the upper left corner of the modal to make sure you have sufficient funds.
4.  **Set the guarantee:** Enter the amount you want to lock as backing. 
    * *Note:* It must be at least the minimum indicated (for example, **100 USDC**).
5.  **Finalize the process:** Click **“Register as Merchant”**.
6.  **Confirmation:** Wait a few seconds until you see the success message. The app will refresh your status automatically.

---

##  Collateral Management

It is important to understand how your guarantee funds are locked:

* **Locked status:** While you hold the merchant role, the collateral will remain locked to secure your operations.
* **Withdraw funds:** If you decide to stop being a merchant, you can use the **“Withdraw Fund”** option.
* **Cooldown period:** There is a waiting time before you can withdraw the full collateral.
    * ⚠️ **72-hour rule:** This preventive lock is only activated if you have performed any buy or sell operation in the last **72 hours**.

> **Tip:** Make sure you have no pending operations before starting the withdrawal process to facilitate the release of your funds.
