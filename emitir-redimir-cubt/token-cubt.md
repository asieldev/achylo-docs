#  CUBT Token

CUBT is a **yield-bearing stablecoin** whose initial value was set according to the protocol's initial liquidity. Since then, its price has gradually appreciated thanks to the yield generated by the protocol's Treasury.

##  Appreciation Mechanism
- Every **28 days**, the Treasury contract allocates **25%** of accumulated funds to increase the collateral backing CUBT.
- This periodic reinforcement increases the value of CUBT against USDC in a **sustainable and transparent** way.
- Result: an asset designed to protect purchasing power and grow alongside the Achylo ecosystem.

---

##  Navigating the “Mint & Redeem” Page
1. From the main menu, open **Mint and Redeem**. You will find two main tabs: **Mint** and **Redeem**.
2. The top panel shows:
   - **USDC** and **CUBT** balances.
   - **Allowance** status.
   - **Refresh** button to query on-chain data again if you changed networks or executed recent transactions.
3. Below you will see a statistics module with total supply, USDC reserves, and current fees. If you have discounts, they appear highlighted in green.

---

##  Use the Simulator Before Operating
When entering an amount:
- In **Mint**, you write how much USDC you want to convert to CUBT.
- In **Redeem**, you indicate how many CUBT you want to redeem for USDC.

The simulator calculates in real time:
1. **Amount to receive** after fees and adjustments.
2. **Standard Fee** (base fee) vs. **Effective Fee** (with your discounts).
3. Whether the **overcollateralization** layer applies.

This allows you to confirm exactly how many tokens you will receive before signing any transaction.

---

##  Quick Tips
- Use **Refresh** if your balance doesn't match the wallet after an operation.
- Check the green badges to confirm active discounts.
- If the simulator indicates overcollateralization, it means that part of the USDC will be locked as additional collateral (according to the current Treasury rules).

With this flow the protocol maintains CUBT stability, avoids surprises for the user, and facilitates secure operations in seconds.
